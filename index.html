CRYSTAL COLLECTOR

Instructions: You will be given a random number at the start of the game. There are four crystals below. By clicking on a crystal you will add a specific amount of points to your total score. You will win the game by matching your total scored to random numbers, you lose the game is your total score goes above or below the random number. The value of each crystal is hidden from you until you click on it. Each time when the game starts, the game will change the values of each crystal.
